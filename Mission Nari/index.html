SafeTransportation


Safety is a major concern raised by women regarding limited access to urban transport; the incidence of gender-based violence is higher for women. A report on Women and Mobility by the Asia Foundation, showed that 50 percent of sexual harassment cases against women in cities took place while using public transport and 16 percent while waiting for public transport. It was also found that poor infrastructure including lack of street lights, public toilets, and useable pavements, made travelling by public transport more uncomfortable and unsafe.

Government Schemes

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao is a flagship program initiated by the Indian government to improve the country's falling girl-child sex ratio. The scheme aims to raise awareness about the importance of girls' education and prevent discrimination against them.The program also promotes girls' health and nutrition while preventing gender-based violence. the government offers cash incentives to families with a girl child.
Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran

The Mahila Kisan Sashaktikaran Pariyojana is an Indian government initiative designed to empower women farmers. The project intends to increase women's participation in agriculture and prepare them for leadership roles in the agricultural sector. Women farmers in this scheme are trained in many parts of farming, including seed production, crop management, and post-harvest management.

Women Helpline Scheme

The Women Helpline Scheme was established by the Indian government to assist women who have been victims of abuse. The project intends to establish a single point of contact for distressed women and ensure that they receive prompt and appropriate support. A national toll-free helpline number (181) has been established under this scheme to assist women who have been victims of violence.
Mahila Samriddhi Yojana

The Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana is an Indian government programme that promotes girl child education and marriage. The program encourages parents to save for their daughter's futures by giving them a safe and secure investment alternative. Parents or legal guardians of a girl child can open a savings account in her name under this scheme.A minimum deposit of Rs. 250 is required to open the account..
Ujjwala Yojana

The Ujjwala Yojana is a scheme introduced by the Indian government to provide free LPG connections to women from low-income households. The project intends to promote clean cooking fuel while improving the health and safety of women historically responsible for domestic cooking. Under this program, qualifying women receive free LPG hookups, a gas stove, and a regulator.The scheme also includes easy installment payment alternatives for LPG refills.
Swadhar Greh

Swadhar Greh is an Indian government initiative that assists women in challenging situations. The program's goal is to give women who are victims of domestic violence, human trafficking, or other forms of abuse safe housing, food, clothing, and counseling. This program provides women with temporary housing and support services such as counseling and legal aid. The program also offers skill development and vocational training to help women become self-sufficient.
Janani Suraksha Yojana

The Janani Suraksha Yojana is a scheme developed by the Indian government to encourage institutional delivery and lower mother and newborn mortality rates. The initiative intends to provide financial help to pregnant women from low-income families to access quality healthcare during pregnancy and childbirth. Under this program, eligible women receive financial support for transportation and medical treatment during pregnancy and childbirth.

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana is an Indian government program that provides financial support to pregnant women and breastfeeding mothers.
